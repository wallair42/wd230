Scenarios

Scenario #1

After 20 years working in Atlanta in the art scene George is looking for a small quite town to purchase and built a art studio that he can continue to create and design his works of art without the busy fast paced environment of a large city. Feels that Olar could be a quite off the grid habitat to do just this.

Scenario #2

Amber is a college student living in Atlanta looking for a fun weekend drive. Has taken some art classes and currently dating an Art MAjor. They want to explorer nad find local new artist that have potential, but also understand the financial restrictions of students. Hoping that a small town with a few artist will be just the fix she needs to get away for the weekend.

Scenario #3

Janet is a 45 year old resident of Columbia South Carolina, recently decided to take up a hobby of visiting small unique towns that have a weird history. Discovers Olar and researches a bit about the history of the area. Feels that this may be a great trip to a small unique town. A little concerned with all fo the arts folks in the area, but still interested.

Scenario #4

Robert, how is know as skippy at home, is looking for a place to retire away from the fast paced life of the Appalachian mountains. Feels that he is getting to old to be driving the mountain roads, but still wants a rural and small town feel, that is close enough to a major metropolitan area for medical access.

Scenario #5

The last is Jeremy, looking for a small town that is cheap to live because the cost of housing in his town are way to high. Want to be within 2 hours of a major city, but can only afford housing that is less than what he gets from selling his home. Came across Olar as one of the smallest cities in South Carolina and is intrigue what resources business, and people are like in the town. May even consider opening a soda shop if the art tourism plan picks up.
